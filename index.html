I am a recently graduated PhD Candidate from Georgia Institute of Technology in the Computational Science and Engineering department! Take a look at my current CV.

My former adviser is Polo Chau and I am an active alumnus of Polo Club of Data Science.

I completed my undergraduate degree in 2011 at Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology in computer science and mathematics.

Summary & Interests

My research blends methods, techniques, and principles from visual analytics and data mining to create new, interactive, scalable tools that empower analysts when exploring, querying, and analyzing large networks.
